If you suspect your network has been compromised, follow these steps in order:
  1. Disconnect the router from the internet.
  2. Factory reset the router and reapply secure settings.
  3. Change all Wi‑Fi and router admin passwords.
  4. Scan connected devices for malware and isolate infected machines.
  5. Review logs for unusual connections or data exfiltration.

Notes & Tips

  • WPA3 is preferred — but verify device compatibility first.
  • IoT devices should go on the guest VLAN or a separate network segment.
  • Keep a secure password manager for router credentials.
